The Story Behind

It all started in 2010 when I was working on aero images and intensively using my coding skills to improve productivity. During that period, I created massive amouts of customized tools for GIS and photogrammetry. Since there were different platforms to work on, I came up with my own way to master a new programming language as quickly as possilbe.

However, as I dug deeper into this field, I found it less attractive and decided to do something more challenging. Therefore, I went back to school and chose computer science as my major. Meanwhile, I also worked as a web developer and was doing some game design just for fun. I had a strong passion for software development at that time, but then a friend of mine introduced me to to an IC design company, and I started to work as a firmware engineer.

While software developers are writing code for humans, firmware people write programs for machines. They are both writing code to achieve certain goals but in different ways. To some extent, I like firmware better because you know, it's based on more basic build blocks. Generally, the more basic it is the more challenging it can be. Perhaps it's just genetic that I'm able to learn difficult things easily or, it's that I love a good challenge!

活在這樣不公平的世界，沒有道理的世界，無規則可循的地方實在是太辛苦了．幸好有一個地方可以實現bug free，讓一切都沒有誤差，也沒有不合邏輯的規則，輸入什麼，輸出就是什麼．公平，有跡可循，符合數學定律的世界，那就是程式的世界．bug free的世界是人類的渴望，現實生活無法滿足的，我們就寫個程式．在這裡沒有不平等，沒有違反數學定律的事情．

We all live in a world full of bugs. Almost every aspect of life is illogical. There are no rules to follow, which makes us live in uncertainty. The cruelest thing on earth is that for those who live in a place full of bug, they are expected to be bug free. No bug is allowed in this world of off-road vehicles. People strive to find their way out under contradictory rules. For example, people who are forced to follow the wrong SOP are usually accused of not following the SOP properly. On the surface, it looks like there are regulations but indeed there are no rules in this world, and it has always been filled with double standards. Everyone struggles to create more bugs in order to explain how this buggy world really works. It's a hard life in this buggy world. However, fortunately, there is a place where bug free can be achieved, a place without errors and illogical rules. A world that is fair, traceable and consistent with the laws of mathematics. Welcome to the world of code. There is no inequality here, no violation of the laws of mathematics. For those who are tired of uncertainty, this is exactly the world you've been longed for.
